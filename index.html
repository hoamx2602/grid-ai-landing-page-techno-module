Platform Features
Everything your battery needs
to earn its keep

GridMind is the intelligence layer that sits between your inverter and the grid — invisible, autonomous, and relentlessly optimising.

🧠

LSTM Neural Network

A Long Short-Term Memory model trained on 3 years of Agile pricing data issues optimised dispatch commands every 30 minutes, adapting to real-time price spikes and drops.

🔌

Universal Inverter API

One-click connection to 14+ inverter brands via the Enode API. Tesla, SolarEdge, Sungrow, Huawei — no custom hardware, no electrician required.

💹

Agile Price Arbitrage

Octopus Agile prices fluctuate from 1.5p to 35p/kWh in a single day. GridMind buys at the floor and sells at the ceiling automatically, capturing spreads of up to 33p.

🏭

Flexibility Service Revenue

Your battery joins the GridMind Virtual Power Plant, earning National Grid ESO Demand Flexibility Service payments of £3–£6 per kWh dispatched during peak events.

📊

Real-Time Dashboard

Live battery state, today's earnings, upcoming price forecasts, and AI decision log — all in a clean mobile-friendly dashboard updated every 30 seconds.

🛡️

Battery Health Guard

AI dispatch respects manufacturer cycle limits and depth-of-discharge rules. Your warranty stays intact; GridMind only trades the headroom that's safe to use.
